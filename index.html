Our Story

Pure, Traditional, and Homely Taste Since 2011

Welcome to Idly Express

Founded on May 22, 2011, by K. Manickam, Idly Express is a traditional South Indian food outlet that specializes exclusively in serving soft, steaming idlies, along with sandhagai and idiyappam — simple, authentic, and healthy dishes loved by all age groups.

At Idly Express, simplicity meets tradition. We focus on limited yet perfect dishes to ensure quality, freshness, and consistency in every plate served. We proudly serve only idlies and traditional items — no sambar. This unique approach helps us maintain the authentic taste and identity that our customers love.

"Authentic Taste of South India — Our commitment to preserving the real flavors of South Indian cuisine, pure, simple, and satisfying."

- K. Manickam, Founder

Our Menu

Simple, Authentic, and Healthy Dishes Loved by All

Idly

Soft, fluffy, and made with freshly ground batter every day. Our signature dish made with traditional fermentation methods, served fresh and warm.

Sandhagai

Light and delicious string hoppers, served fresh and warm. A traditional South Indian delicacy that captures the essence of authentic breakfast culture.

Idiyappam

Traditional steamed rice noodles, soft and healthy. A classic South Indian breakfast favorite, prepared with care and served fresh daily.
